CODE

The roclin group is a CODE partnership: a Centrally Organized group of Decentralized Entities.

We are developing a nonlinear media platform to revolutionize the filter, improve search quality and health. Our method is aimed to solve the problem of third party censorship, bias and fake news; finding information when you're not an expert in what good search results look like, and to manipulate algorithm promoting results. This innovation implemented over a decentralized network will serve to make indexing and machine learning not only feasible but beneficial to the rights and privacy of data traveling through a more respectable, secure and sensible Internet of Things.
